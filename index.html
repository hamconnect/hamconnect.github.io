What is a Ham Connect?

      A Ham Connect is an opportunity for Ham Radio operators to learn how to use their knowledge to participate more fully in the world of ham radio. This includes activities such as contesting, getting on HF, outdoor activities like Parks on the Air (POTA) or Summits on the AIR (SOTA), DIY antenna building, and how to prepare for and participate in emergency communication (EmComm) either from home or in the field. This preparation often involves fun events that provide a community service while expanding radio skills. Ham operators will meet other experienced operators from a variety of local organizations.

Participating Organizations:

  • Seattle Auxiliary Communications Service (ACS)
  • Seatttle Emergency Communication Hubs
  • Puget Sound Repeater Group (PSRG)
  • Mike and Key Amateur Radio Club
  • West Seattle Amateur Radio Club (WSARC)
  • Cascadia Radio
  • West Washington Medical Services Team (WW7MST)
